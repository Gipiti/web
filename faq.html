F.A.QPages

Shipping Information

How will my parcel be delivered?

Your parcel will be delivered via the shipping method you selected during checkout. We partner with reliable carriers to ensure timely and safe delivery of your items. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Do I pay for delivery?

Delivery charges vary based on the shipping method selected and the destination of your order. Shipping fees are calculated at checkout and are displayed before you finalize your purchase. We also offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount.

Will I be charged customs fees?

Customs fees may apply for international shipments based on your country's import regulations. These fees are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in the purchase price or shipping cost. We recommend checking with your local customs office for more information.

My item has become faulty

We apologize for any inconvenience caused. If your item is faulty, please contact our customer support within 30 days of receiving your order. We will assist you with a replacement or a full refund based on our return policy.

Orders and Returns

Tracking my order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. Use this number on our carrier’s website to monitor the status and location of your parcel in real-time.

I haven’t received my order

If you haven't received your order within the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer support team. Provide your order number and any relevant details so we can assist you in locating your package.

How can I return an item?

To return an item, please log into your account, navigate to your orders, and select the item you wish to return. Follow the instructions to generate a return label. Ensure the item is in its original condition and packaging before sending it back.

Payments

What payment types can I use?

We accept a variety of payment methods including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Choose the option that is most convenient for you during the checkout process.

Can I pay by Gift Card?

Yes, you can use our gift cards as a payment method. During checkout, select the gift card option and enter your gift card code to apply the balance to your order.

I can't make a payment

If you're experiencing issues with making a payment, please ensure that your payment method details are correct and that you have sufficient funds. If the problem persists, contact your bank or our customer support team for further assistance.

Has my payment gone through?

After completing your payment, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard under "Orders". If you haven't received a confirmation, please contact our customer support.
